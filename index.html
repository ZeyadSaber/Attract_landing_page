Introductory Video

Attract is a 360 comprehensive, full-service marketing, advertising and web development firm based in Alexandria - Egypt and United Arab Emirates - Dubai. Established in 2022 with a talented team has more than 15 years of deep experience in all media and aspects of marketing and advertising.

Inspired by the law of attraction, which states that positive energy attracts positive things into our lives, governs our thinking and actions, and how we can use the power of positive thinking to achieve anything we can imagine.

The digital era has made significant changes in the business field. The rising value of digital marketing and social media and the urge to keep up with the digital revolution gave us the necessary motivation to start co. We have proudly gathered a loyal team that Attract is expanding every day.
